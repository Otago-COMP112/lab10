Recipe #1 Chocolate Rough Base Ingredients: 110g butter 1 cup sugar 2 tsp cocoa 2½ tsp golden syrup 1 cup flour 1 tsp baking powder ¾ cup coconut Icing Ingredients: 1 cup icing sugar 25g melted butter 2 tsp cocoa ¾ cup coconut A little water to combine Cream butter and sugar together. Add golden syrup. Add sifted cocoa, flour and baking powder. Add coconut. Combine and press into a shallow tin. Bake at 180°c for 20 - 30 minutes. Ice while still warm. To make icing: sift icing sugar and cocoa together, stir through coconut and then add butter and enough water to mix to a stiff paste.
